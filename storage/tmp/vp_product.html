La France Héraldique, Album I

Nepoznati autori

Sažetak

First album of La France Héraldique series of albums issued by the Café Sanka company, the French daughter of the German Kaffee Hag company.It contains images of cities in the first four regions only. All other regions are only shown with the arms of the region. Issued as  loose sheets, bound together. In good condition. Three stamps, of the arms of the regions Ile de France,  Flandre and Alsace, are missing

For more info: https://www.heraldry-wiki.com/heraldrywiki/index.php?title=Caf%C3%A9_Sanka_:_La_France_H%C3%A9raldique                   https://www.heraldry-wiki.com/heraldrywiki/index.php?title=Sanka_Album_1
